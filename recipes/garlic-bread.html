Your Grandma's Favorite Garlic Bread

Description

This recipe is one of my favorites and my grandma's favorite. She makes it every time we have some sort of pasta dish. It is a very simple and easy dish.

Ingredients

  • Really any bread you would like, but we recommend tuscan
  • Minced Garlic
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • Rosemerry

Steps

  1. First, mince your garlic and thinly chop rosemerry
  2. Next, combine minced garlic, butter, and rosemerry in a bowl
  3. Brush the combined ingredients over the bread
  4. Bake until crispy
  5. Lastly, brush any remaining mix over the bread then enjoy!
