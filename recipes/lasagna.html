The Absolute Sauciest Lasagna EVER!

Description

This Lasagna is basically soup. If you like sauce and just some bits a pieces then this recipe is for you. This recipe has a rich sauce with really nothing else.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sauce from the recipe on this site
  • Cut up meatballs from the recipe for meatballs on this site
  • Basil
  • Cheese
  • One bar of lasagna pasta

Steps

  1. First, cook the one bar of pasta
  2. Next, cut up the basil and mix it into the sauce
  3. Almost lastly, pull out a pan and combine all the ingredients
  4. Lastly, put cheese over mix and bake
