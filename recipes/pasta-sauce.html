The Creamiest Pasta Sauce You'll Ever Cream!

Description

This pasta sauce is so watery yet creamy it's an impossible texture. So weird you might puke, but I guess it's good...

Ingredients

  • pre-made pasta sauce
  • water
  • half and half

Steps

  1. First, pour all the pre-made pasta sauce into a big pot.
  2. Next, boil all the water you want to use.
  3. Then, pour both the boiling water and the half and half carton into the pasta sauce.
  4. Lastly, boil it then serve!
